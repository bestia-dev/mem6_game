"unForGetTable"

a drinking game
to lose memory

Instructions

This multi-player game is prepared for android smartphones with chrome browser and internet connection. All players must be around the same table. It is best played with 4 players or more.

The first player opens the link

bestia.dev/mem6

On the start page writes his nickname. Clicks `Start group`. On the next page is a qr code. Other players scan this qr code. It will open the browser on the game's webpage. Write your nicknames. Put all phones down on the table near each other. All smartphones combined will be used as a single game board. The first player then chooses the type of game and clicks

Start "unForGet"

On the screen under the card grid are information who plays and who waits. Each player flips over two cards with two clicks. These cards can be on any smartphone. The cards are accompanied by sounds and text on the screen.

Put your volume up!

If the cards match, they are left face up permanently and the player receives a point and

MUST DRINK !

The player then clicks on the green page to let the next player play. If the cards don't match, they will be later flipped back face down. Every player opens only two cards (to make the game faster). It doesn't matter if he got a point or not. Then it is always the next player's turn to click and flip over two cards.
And so the next and the next and the next...
The game is over when all the cards are permanently face up.
Good luck !

Enjoy drinking

and the good company!
